What is Findteddy?

Findteddy is a beacon app that lets your kids find their beloved teddy without a hassle! Our built in mini-games will make the search an even more fun experience!

So how does it work?

A necklace with a beacon is connected to your device and always remembers the last place your kid has put his teddy. The beacon will even light up when it's dark out!
